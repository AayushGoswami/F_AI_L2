About Us

Welcome to our AI-powered assistant, a collaborative creation by Aayush Goswami and Om Modi. This platform is designed specifically for doctors, offering a streamlined way to summarize critical information from medical reports. By simply uploading a PDF or an image of a medical report, our intelligent system quickly processes the data and provides concise, accurate summaries to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions.

This current version is a Prototype, showcasing the potential of AI in simplifying medical documentation and analysis. While it focuses on summarizing medical reports, it has been designed with scalability in mind. Our platform is a stepping stone toward a more comprehensive solution that can handle complex medical documentation effortlessly.
